Why this architecture

Rule engine first
Hard signals hold veto power
A sanctions hit, a high-risk country, or repeated applications from the same EIN are automatic blocks. Deterministic rules never lie — the LLM is not consulted for these cases.
LLM adds judgment
Context the rules can't see
Claude reads the full application and rule results, then writes a structured risk assessment: a 0–100 score, recommended action, top concerns, and a rationale you can audit later.
Human stays in the loop
Disagreement routes to you
If rules and the LLM significantly disagree — or if the combined score falls in the grey zone — the application lands in your queue. You see the full dossier and make the call.
